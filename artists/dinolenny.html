Dino Lenny

dinolenny

Dino Lenny (R&S/ Ellum/ Afterlife/ Diynamic/ Innervisons/ Cocoon) London Dino Lenny is an Italian electronic producer settled in London He is the founder of Fine Human Records. Starts young as a dj in a small radio in Cassino (Italy). After a while he moves to London where he gaines notoriety remixing Underworld, INXS, Missy Elliot, Timbaland and collaborating with everyone from Scissor Sisters to WuTang Clan and Madonna. From 2014 onwards starts enjoying successful releases on labels like Ellum, Innerv isions, Crosstown Rebels, Correspondant, Diynamic and many others.
